HIERARCHY

An abstract, strategic card game for two players

Designed by Hugo Kawamata.
Art by Daniel Ido.
Published by Button Shy Games.

HOW TO PLAY

Cards must be played atop those with lesser value unless the top card or the card to be played alters this rule. When the active player cannot play a card, their opponent wins. The player dealt the Imposter starts.

Click a card to inspect it. Drag a card to the line to play it. Highlighted cards are valid plays.

The surgeon cannot return discarded cards or the tower (counts as two cards), and the sorcerer's effect expires once it is no longer the top card.
