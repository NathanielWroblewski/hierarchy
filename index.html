HIERARCHY

An abstract, strategic card game.

Cards must be played atop those with lesser value unless the last card or the current card alters this rule. When the active player cannot play a card, their opponent wins. The player dealt the Imposter starts.

Click a card to inspect it. Drag a card to the line to play it. Highlighted cards are valid plays.
